Selecting

We make a standard about choosing which farm to grow our coffee cherries and also in the choosing bean process. This process is not only about our standard but the wish of us to serve the greatest coffee to our customer.

Roasting

Roasting causes chemical changes to take place as the beans are rapidly brought to very high temperatures. When they reach the peak of perfection, they are quickly cooled to stop the process.

Brewing

Brewing coffee is the process of extracting the soluble material in roasted and ground coffee. As this coffee is brewed in hot water, hundreds of unique compounds are extracted from the ground beans – creating brewed coffee.

Drinking

Drinking in the day’s first sip of coffee is like paradise massaging the taste buds. It is, as one coffee-in-a-can company figured out long ago, the best part of waking up.
